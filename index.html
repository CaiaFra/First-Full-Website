Porto, Portugal

The city that enchants everyone from the first time. The beautiful views over the river from its seven bridges are stunning. Anytime of the day you'd be impressed with what the city has to offer, from food to culture.

Things to see or try in the city

Take a tram to riveira
Enjoy some local pasteis de nata with coffee
Visit the local churches and tiles
Take a picture of the sunset with a loved one
This city is amazing. It fascinates everyone who goes and leaves the feeling of "saudades" when leaving.
-Francesco, lifetime traveler
